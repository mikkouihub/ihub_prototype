Filceb Elevate
The FilCeb Elevate program’s mission is to empower entrepreneurs with the suitable tools, accountability, and community to aggressively grow and master their business.
As a budding entrepreneur, you’re bound to face new challenges every day. You’re expected to face a grueling task of fulfilling new orders. You also have to strategize on how you’re going to penetrate new markets. Most importantly, you also need to figure out how you’re going to make your business more profitable. Filceb Mentorship
FilCeb Mentorship program upholds Mentor-Mentee relationships which aim to develop high-level leadership and personal goals within a structured time frame.  All throughout the mentorship program, the mentees are required to work toward their goals and establish personal accountability. On the other hand, the mentors are expected to offer support through next-level education and engagement.
